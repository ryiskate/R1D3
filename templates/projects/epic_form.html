Epic Guidelines
What is an Epic?

An epic is a large body of work that can be broken down into smaller tasks. Think of it as a major feature or story.

Examples:
  • Game Development: "Create Main Character"
  • Education: "Launch Python Course"
  • Social Media: "Q4 Marketing Campaign"
Best Practices:
  • Keep epic titles clear and concise
  • Set realistic target dates
  • Assign an owner to track progress
  • Break down into manageable tasks
  • Update status regularly
